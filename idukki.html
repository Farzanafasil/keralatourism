IDUKKI

About

Munnar Town is a heart throbbing town. It is also a popular hill station which is located in Idukki lying southwestern state of Kerala. This hill station is situated 1600 meters approximately above the sea level. The range of Western Ghats meets the border line of Munnar Town. Natural sightseeing and exotic tourist spots in Munnar boost up tourists to explore this hill station whenever they find time to spend vacation. Munnar is the confluence of three major rivers namely Kundaly, Mudhirapuzha, and Nallathanni. Pleasant climate, majestic scenery of steep cliffs and nonchalant blue canopy spreading over the extensive green tea garden revive the true romantic ethos to enchant sweethearts to stay in this hill station.
